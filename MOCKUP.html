Hi there! I'm your AI tutor. How was your day?

It was great! I went to the beach with my friends. We have a good time.

Quick correction!

You wrote: "We have a good time."

A better way to say this is: "We had a good time," since you're talking about the past.

AI Feedback & Learning Points

Grammar: Tense

Incorrect phrase: "I have go to the park."

Suggestion: "I have gone to the park."

Explanation: When using the present perfect tense with 'have', you should use the past participle form of the verb. 'Gone' is the past participle of 'go'.

Vocabulary: Awkward Phrasing

Original phrase: "The weather was very nice."

Suggestion: "The weather was beautiful." or "It was a gorgeous day."

Explanation: While 'nice' is correct, using more descriptive words like 'beautiful' or 'gorgeous' makes your writing more vivid and engaging.
